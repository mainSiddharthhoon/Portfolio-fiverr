CSE Student & Frontend Developer

I'm a third-year Computer Science student from India with a deep passion for building premium web experiences. For several years I've been exploring software development — driven by curiosity, creativity, and a desire to turn ideas into meaningful digital products.

I specialize in modern web development — creating visually stunning, responsive, and performant websites for businesses, startups, and creators. My stack includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GSAP animations, and Spline 3D, combined with a strong eye for design and user experience.

By blending hand-coded development with AI-assisted workflows, I deliver high-quality solutions efficiently — without sacrificing flexibility, customization, or attention to detail. My goal: help clients establish a premium online presence that reflects their brand and drives results.
